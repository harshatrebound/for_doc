Foot comprises of 26 bones. These include tarsal bones, metacarpals and phalanges the toe bones. Foot is a complex structure with arches from front to back as well as inner to outer side. This complex supple structure helps foot to work as a shock absorber and transmits minimal forces upwards to the ankle and further. Foot conditions can be due to injury, stress or a multitude of other conditions including diabetes, vascular problems etc.
