About Us
Sports Orthopedics Institute is one of the premier healthcare institutions in India, providing appropriate care for the patients with diverse medical problems such as sports injuries, arthritis, trauma, ligament tears and more. The Centre provides super-specialty care for all the orthopedic and sports injuries.
The Expert Team over here is headed by Dr Naveen Kumar LV. He has been well supported by Dr. Sameer and rest of the team. A well qualified team of Surgeons work with Dr Naveen Kumar tirelessly to provide the best service for the patients.
The team is associated with Manipal Hospitals, Sarjapur Road and performs surgeries over there. Dr Naveen Kumar LV is also involved in a lot of teaching activities and he takes pride in training young orthopedic surgeons.
The team performs thousands of Arthroscopies, Arthroplasties (Joint Replacement), ligament reconstructions such as ACL Reconstruction, meniscal repairs, tendon repairs, rotator cuff repair, Bankarts repair, chondroplasty, stem cell therapy, AVN Hip surgery, bone fracture fixation, trauma surgeries and so on.

MBBS, MS Orth (India), FRCS Orth (Eng), MCh Hip & Knee (UK), MSc Orth (UK), Dip SICOT (Italy), FEBOT (Portugal), MRCGP (UK), Dip FIFA SM (Switzerland) (FSEM (UK))
